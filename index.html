About Ranchu's

The Ranchu goldfish, nicknamed the "king of goldfish", is a stunning fish known for its egg-shaped body, deep belly, and lack of a dorsal fin. They also have a unique head growth called a hood, which adds to their majestic appearance. Despite their fancy looks, they are surprisingly hardy fish.
